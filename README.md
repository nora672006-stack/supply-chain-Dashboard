📊 Supply Chain Dashboard
📌 Overview
The Supply Chain Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide a clear and comprehensive view of logistics, product performance, and operations. It enables decision-makers to monitor key metrics, evaluate inventory and shipping performance, and analyze supply chain efficiency through interactive visualizations.
🎯 Objectives
 Monitor overall supply chain and logistics performance.
 Track total revenue, stock levels, and product sales.
 Analyze lead times and shipping costs.
 Evaluate product availability and distribution across routes and locations.
 Support data-driven decision-making through interactive reports.
